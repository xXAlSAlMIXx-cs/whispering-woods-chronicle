After weeks of winding paths and riddles whispered by wind and stone, Leira stood at last before a shimmering spring hidden deep within the heart of the woods. Guided by the map’s final glyph, she spoke the ancient words etched in moss-covered stone—and the spring burst to life, flowing with crystal water imbued with healing magic. When she returned to the village, the once-wilted fields flourished, and sickness faded like mist in sunlight. The villagers hailed her as a hero, but Leira only smiled, her hand resting on Shadow’s fur. She had followed curiosity—and found a future brighter than any legend.
